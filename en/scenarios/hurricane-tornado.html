Scenario

Hurricane / Tornado

Step 1: Shelter in a small interior room on the lowest level.
Step 2: Stay away from windows and exterior doors.
Step 3: Protect your head and neck with a mattress or sturdy object.
Step 4: Wait for the all-clear; do not go out during calm periods.
Step 5: Check for injuries and watch for downed power lines.
Step 6: Use a radio to follow official instructions and updates.
