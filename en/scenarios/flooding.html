Scenario

Flooding

Step 1: Move to higher ground immediately and avoid basements.
Step 2: Do not walk or drive through floodwater; it can be fast and contaminated.
Step 3: Turn off electricity at the breaker if the area is dry and safe.
Step 4: Stay away from downed power lines and submerged hazards.
Step 5: Use a battery radio for evacuation routes and safe shelters.
Step 6: Drink sealed or treated water; boil if water safety is uncertain.
Step 7: Document damage only when it is safe to do so.
