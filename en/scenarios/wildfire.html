Scenario

Wildfire

Step 1: Move upwind and away from smoke and embers.
Step 2: If indoors, close windows and doors to reduce smoke.
Step 3: Cover nose and mouth with a cloth if air is smoky.
Step 4: Be ready to evacuate quickly with essentials.
Step 5: Avoid driving through heavy smoke or fire paths.
Step 6: Use a radio for evacuation routes and updates.
