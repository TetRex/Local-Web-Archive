Scenario

Bombardment

Step 1: Shelter in the lowest interior area or underground if possible.
Step 2: Stay away from windows and exterior walls.
Step 3: Protect your head and neck with sturdy cover.
Step 4: Wait for the all-clear before moving.
Step 5: Avoid unexploded objects; do not touch them.
Step 6: Check for injuries and use first aid.
Step 7: Use a radio for official updates and safety routes.
