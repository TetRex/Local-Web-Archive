Scenario

Earthquake

Step 1: Check yourself and others for injuries; give first aid if needed.
Step 2: Move away from damaged buildings, glass, and downed power lines.
Step 3: Expect aftershocks; stay in an open area or a safe interior spot.
Step 4: If you smell gas or hear hissing, shut off gas if trained and safe to do so.
Step 5: Use stairs, not elevators, and watch for falling debris.
Step 6: Use a battery radio for updates and instructions.
Step 7: Prepare water, light, and essentials for several hours.
