Scenario

Power Plant Incident

Step 1: Shelter indoors and close windows, doors, and vents.
Step 2: Turn off fans and HVAC systems that bring outside air in.
Step 3: Use sealed food and water; avoid exposed supplies.
Step 4: Stay away from the affected area and avoid travel.
Step 5: Use a battery radio for official instructions.
Step 6: Wash hands and face if you were outdoors.
