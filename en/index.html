Archive Overview

Earthquake

  • Check injuries and immediate hazards.
  • Stay alert for aftershocks.

Flooding

  • Move to higher ground.
  • Avoid floodwater.

Hurricane / Tornado

  • Shelter away from windows.
  • Wait for the all-clear.

Wildfire

  • Move upwind from smoke.
  • Prepare to evacuate.

Power Plant Incident

  • Shelter indoors.
  • Follow radio instructions.

Cyberattack / Infrastructure Failure

  • Use offline notes and radio.
  • Conserve battery.

Bombardment

  • Shelter low and inside.
  • Avoid windows.

Sabotage / Suspicious Activity

  • Avoid affected areas.
  • Keep distance from objects.

Blockade / Restricted Movement

  • Ration supplies.
  • Limit travel.

Ice Storm

  • Stay indoors.
  • Avoid travel.

Extreme Heat

  • Hydrate often.
  • Cool down regularly.

Extreme Cold

  • Layer clothing.
  • Warm one room.

Planned / Emergency Outages

  • Use flashlights.
  • Conserve power.

Economic Crisis

  • Secure essentials.
  • Track official updates.
